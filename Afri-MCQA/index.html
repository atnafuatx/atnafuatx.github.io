Core Goals

  • Cultural validity: questions reflect authentic local practices, artifacts, and language usage.
  • Linguistic breadth: multiple African languages, dialectal variants where feasible.
  • Fair evaluation: perâ€‘language and perâ€‘category breakdowns, plus overall scores.

  • Traditions & social practices

Dataset Construction

  1. Sourcing: community contributors, local photographers, and permissive repositories (e.g., CCâ€‘BY/CC0). Sensitive content excluded by policy.
  2. Authoring: native speakers craft MCQA items (A/B/C/D) and rationales; ambiguity checks added.
  3. Validation: crossâ€‘lingual verification and pilot studies; interâ€‘annotator agreement reported.
  4. Splits: train/dev/test with careful entity and region deâ€‘duplication to reduce leakage.

  • Representation: balancing across regions, cultures, and urban/rural scenes where feasible.
